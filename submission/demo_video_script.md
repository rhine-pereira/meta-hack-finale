# GENESIS 90-Second Demo Script

## 0:00-0:10 - Hook

"Ninety-five percent of startups fail, mostly because coordination fails before the product does. GENESIS trains AI co-founders to run a company from Day 0 to Series A over 540 simulated business days."

## 0:10-0:25 - Environment Overview

"Five specialized agents - CEO, CTO, Sales, People, and CFO - operate with partial observability. They use 28 MCP tools to ship features, hire, raise, communicate with customers, and pivot when needed."

## 0:25-0:45 - Realism and Difficulty

"This is not a toy loop. Actions have delayed side effects: job postings and hires resolve over time, and strategic mistakes can cascade weeks later. In Gauntlet mode, agents face weekly personal crises while balancing growth, morale, and runway."

## 0:45-1:05 - Reward and Learning Signal

"GENESIS uses an 11-component rubric covering valuation, runway, retention, morale, co-founder alignment, crisis handling, and pivot quality. This prevents single-metric gaming and rewards durable strategy."

## 1:05-1:20 - Evidence

"Our training pipeline produces reproducible reward artifacts. You can inspect reward curves and summary metrics directly in the repo. Current runs show clear measurable progress and stable evaluation outputs."

## 1:20-1:30 - Close

"GENESIS is an OpenEnv-compliant startup simulator for training long-horizon, multi-agent business intelligence. Run it on Hugging Face Space, inspect the tools, and watch AI teams learn to build companies."
